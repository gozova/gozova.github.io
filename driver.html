Why drive with Gozova?

Drivers are treated like customers at Gozova. Every driver is crucial to our business and we want to make sure you're taken care of. Unlike other on-demand logistics services, we don't take a large percentage of the fare. Gozova wants to be a truck driver's friend and we made the process simple to become part of the team.

Gozova is a simple app that connects people in need of a truck to haul their items with drivers (AKA you) who meet their needs. You arrive in a pickup truck, the customer loads their items and the you know exactly where to go. Think of it as renting your truck out but you drive it for them. This saves money for the customers and ensures that you won't have to handle the heavy lifting if you don't want to.

No cash transactions, everything is done within the app. You'll get paid through a secure online system and you'll be able to see how much money you're making.

How is this better for me?

Flex Hours

Have a day job and need some extra cash? Or maybe you are self employed and are able to pickup jobs during the day. Either way we can give you this flexibility since you work when you can.

Guaranteed compensation

Ever had someone tell you they'll pay you back and they never do? Well you won't have to worry about that. We ensure that you'll get compensated for your time even if the request is cancelled, you'll get paid for the time spent responding to that request.

Friendly Company

We take pride in being available to drivers 24-7 should you have any issues or concerns. We know how annoying it is to be put on hold or to send an email and get a delayed response.

Great pay

How it works for drivers

If you haven't already submitted your application to be a driver, please do so HERE. Once you're accepted we will send you to a third party to connect your bank account to our payroll system.

As soon as that's all set, simply open up the app and login. Then you can set your status to available in order to receive requests from customers. As soon as a request appears you will have 20 seconds to accept or it will go to the next driver.

The app will show your pickup and dropoff points, describe the items, and the customer. The app will allow you to set your current status so the customer can know that someone is headed their way. You, the driver, aren't required to help load or unload the truck but if you do help, it might help you get a positive review and maybe even a tip!

Throughout the entire drive, there will be several times where both you and the customer will acknowledge or approve certain stages of the pickup. This is to help both you and the customer stay on the same page and make sure everyone is happy.

You will be paid as soon as you request for the money you've earned. It takes at least a few days to process the transaction with your bank so keep that in mind.
