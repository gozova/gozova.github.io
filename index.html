About

What is Gozova?

Gozova is a simple app that connects people in need of a truck to haul their items with drivers who meet their demands. Drivers arive in a pickup truck, you load your items and the driver knows exactly where to go.

No cash transactions, everything is done within the app.

How it works

Simply open up the app, create an account or login with an existing one. Then you can easily request a truck driver immediately or schedule one for a specific time. Pick your pickup location and your destination and make sure you have someone to help you load your stuff. We will dispatch the driver in time to make sure they are on time.

It's easy for users to sign up and to start using the service. We pride ourselves in customer service and satisfaction. Everyone wants a good deal and good service which is what we aim to provide. Simply select your start and end points, describe your items, and pick a date or request instantly. The driver will accept the request and you'll be notified when they are on the way. The driver isn't required to help load or unload the truck but if they do help, you might want to thank them with a positive review and maybe even a tip!
