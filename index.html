About

What is Gozova?

Gozova is a mobile app that connects you to a truck when you need it. Request a pickup on demand to drivers in the area. Drivers arrive in a pickup truck, you load your items and the driver knows where to go.

No cash transactions, everything is done within the app.
All items insured up to $20,000

How it works

Simply open up the app, create an account or login. Then you can easily request a truck driver immediately or schedule one for a later time. Pick your pickup location and your destination and make sure you have someone to help you load your stuff. We will dispatch the driver in time to make sure they arrive when needed.

It's easy for users to sign up and to start using the service. We pride ourselves in customer service and satisfaction. Everyone wants a good deal and good service which is what we aim to provide. Simply select your start and end points, describe your items, and pick a date or request instantly. The driver will accept the request and you'll be notified when they are on the way. The driver isn't required to help load or unload the truck but if they do help, you might want to thank them with a positive review and maybe even a tip!

Drivers are treated like customers at Gozova. Every driver is crucial to our business and we want to make sure you're taken care of. Unlike other on-demand logistics services, we don't take a large percentage of the fare. Gozova wants to be a truck driver's friend and we made the process simple to become part of the team.
